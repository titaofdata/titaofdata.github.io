Getting merchants to sign up as partner stores and having been incubated @ Impact Hub Asia, our team made improvements to the app, as seen below:

Role

As co-founder of Kuptura, I was responsible for finding product-market fit, CX, wireframing, market research and business development.

As part of the Impact Hub Asia's incubation program for startups building a solution against plastic pollution, Team STP had mentors for all things startup - from lean management to setting up finances and legal (IP rights, LLC, etc).

This experience helped us navigate the first few months of establishing our startup in PH.

In addition, while we were incubatees @ Impact Hub Asia, I met with community leaders in underprivileged areas (BASECO) and other zero-waste startup founders to improve our tech and product further.
