Future of Decision-making

Terraza Data is offering data science services to help SMEs and solo entrepreneurs find the next best step through analytics consulting and DS training.

Recently, we have helped researchers from Seoul to build a prototype that would help address students' needs amidst the pandemic while they take classes online.

How we did it:

  • Epistemic Network Analysis
  • Data Engineering
  • K-means Clustering
  • Human-assisted Semi-automated Data Annotation
  • Multi-class Classification

Before that, we have worked with an e-commerce company to perform market research, pricing analysis, customer-behavior-based marketing campaigns, and build a high-level end-to-end data architecture for the client.

How we did it:

  • Web scraping
  • Customer Segmentation (purchase behavior & psychographics)
  • Data Storytelling
  • ETL, Data Warehouse, Dashboard
  • Metric design & development
