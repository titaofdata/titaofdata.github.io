Role

For this startup, I was a coinventor, business developer and tech maven working on market validation, software and hardware debugging, and improvements using machine learning. To ensure continuity of the product, we collaborated with other biomedical instrumentation startups and served as mentor to Innovation Research Center undergrad apprentices. Our team pursued the venture outside the campus and have been a finalist in Pilantropo (Metro Manila Leg) and Tagcash Pitch Party.
