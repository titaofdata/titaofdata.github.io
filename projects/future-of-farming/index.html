Future Fresh

At Future Fresh, we marry technology and a love of fresh food to provide our customers the best in quality. We offer same-day delivery of fresh produce urban-farmed, harvested hours away. Bringing you a true seed-to-plate experience.


Role

My role involved providing leadership and direction to the IoT and Data team, as well as adopting a hands-on approach in problem solving, development and deployment of technologies for farming operations.

Product Management x Agri-tech

As a product manager at NXTLVL Farms, my team and I built technologies to solve challenges we face at the farm, such as:

  • AIoT-enabled Hydroponic Environmental Monitoring & Control System, in collaboration with IoT Engineering team
  • Pest Detection with Integrated Messaging to Alert farm hands on-site when a harmful pest is detected
  • Software Development, Data Management & Cloud Migration to a Centralized Data Warehouse to improve data integrity and overall data quality, worked with Software Engineering team
  • Yield Forecast using nutrient and dose-response analysis, in collaboration with in-house agronomist & Chemical engineer
  • Topic Modelling & Sentiment Analysis on Customer Messages, in collaboration with Marketing team
